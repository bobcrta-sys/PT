Checklist Items
  • Bring in any teaching equipment that may have been left out in the teaching area
  • Sweep the floor in the PT building and mop floor underneath the ball tray and cleaner. Pick up any deer scat with a dustbroom and throw in the woods — do not throw out on the rocks by the pump at the back door
  • Empty all garbage cans (inside building, behind stalls, and inside Golf Team area)
  • Check the condition of the mats and rotate them 1/4 turn in the rubber mat if it shows heavy wear
  • Check the condition of the hitting panels and replace as needed; also seat in mat base
  • Straighten and properly set mats into each depression in the rubber base
  • Make sure that all the mats have two heights of rubber tees
  • Ask customers to empty balls into ball holder and retrieve their baskets and any loose baskets
  • Pick up extra balls in trays and give to other patrons or put in hopper
  • Put the metal bucket stand and buckets in the garage together — do not remove buckets from stand and leave the stand outside overnight
  • Put back any supplies that were used during the day
  • Clean the top of the bench
  • Break down any cardboard boxes and store on shelf
  • Remove tokens from both ball dispensers and turn off the machine at the toggle switch — tokens are to be delivered to the pro shop front desk
  • Remove the drain hose and hang it on the ball washer
  • Park the range picker pointing out towards the back of the range, away from the PT building
  • Disconnect the range picker from the cart and back the cart into the building
  • Close the garage door and make sure that the entrance door closes and locks
  • Lock the bathroom doors
  • Pull set of chains across by parking lot
  • Pull the gate across the ball machine and lock top lock
